First run: build your profile

build my voice profile
A short interview over 3 to 5 pieces of writing you consider most you. Produces Voice_Profile.md: your authority, hedging, texture, sentence mechanics, structure, and tone, every marker backed by a quoted sentence of yours. Lives in your folder, never leaves it.

Voice check

does this sound like me voice check this draft
  • Fingerprint: the 3 sentences most and least like you, quoted
  • Seven dimensions scored 1 to 5, each with cited evidence
  • Every AI tell flagged, with the specific tell named
  • Density signal: flagged constructions per 100 words (warn above 2.0), plus cluster warnings per paragraph
  • Verdict: PASS REVISE REWRITE

The seven dimensions

1Groundingwhere your authority comes from
2Calibrationhow you hedge, how confident you get
3Textureyour concrete specifics
4Mechanicsrhythm, punctuation, fragments, humor
5AI artifactsthe shared tell catalog (right)
6Structurehow you open, build, end; what stays implicit
7Tonethe overall feel, and what you never sound like

Targeted rewrites

Arrive with any REVISE or REWRITE verdict. Only the flagged sentences are rewritten, each fix explained. Facts, names, numbers, dates, sources, and emphasis stay exactly as you had them; terms of art survive.

Correct a flag

that one is actually me
The correction appends to your profile with the sentence as evidence. The validator gets more accurate the more you use it.

Tell categories at a glance

5afiller and intensifiers ("crucial," "delve," "leverage")
5bflattened stock phrases ("paradigm shift")
5cantithesis family ("It doesn't just X. It Y.")
5dscene-setting openers ("In a world where...")
5eengagement bait ("here's the thing")
5ftriplet noun piling ("clarity, trust, and growth")
5gcounter-X constructions
5hauthenticity paradox ("to be honest," "frankly")
5istructural artifacts: em dashes, hedge-stacking, canned closers, loaded adverbs
Quotes, technical-literal uses, and single isolated instances are exempt. Density and clustering are the tell.

CLI linter (no model needed)

python3 tools/voicelint.py draft.md
  • Mechanical tells with exact line and column numbers
  • --json --strict for CI or a pre-commit hook
  • Exits 0 clean, 1 findings, 2 usage or IO error
  • Reads Markdown, plain text, and HTML; stdlib only, Python 3.8+

Personal linter config

make me a linter config from my profile
Your dash stance, crutch-word caps, and extra bans as a voice_config.json the CLI linter can run anywhere.

About the defaults

The shipped rules are the author's own, built from tells observed across many AI-assisted documents, and they ship strict. Your register may differ: edit tools/voice_config.json to your purpose, or relax single rules in your own config (tools/examples/relaxed.json shows the shape), with the override recorded in your profile so both layers agree.
